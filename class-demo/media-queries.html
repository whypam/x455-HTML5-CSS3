Resize the browser window to see the color changes

When the viewport is 800 pixels or wider, the background-color is lightblue. When the viewport is between 600 and 799 pixels, the background-color is pink, When the viewport is less than 599 or less pixels wide, the background-color is lightgreen However, if the viewport is landscape orientation, the background-color is yellow.
